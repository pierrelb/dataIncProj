Expanded financial resources in modern soccer

The FIFA World Cup takes place every 4 years, and the next edition will be in Russia in the summer of 2018. Only Westen European and South American nations have participated in the finals of all World Cups, but other nations have made significant investments in the sport hoping for improved performances at the World Cup. The investment in the last 3 decades has had an impact in professional soccer, but it's effects on the international game is less understood.
