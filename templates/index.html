Expanded financial resources in modern football

The FIFA World Cup takes place every 4 years, and the next edition will be in Russia in the summer of 2018. Only Westen European and South American nations have participated in the finals of all World Cups, but other nations have made significant investments in the sport hoping for improved performances at the World Cup. This is an investigation into the factors affecting performance at World Cup Tournaments.
