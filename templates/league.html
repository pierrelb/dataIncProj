Consistent qualification

Soccer teams that have consistently qualified for World Cups tend to fare better at the tournament. The data below shows the average World Cup position of the 50 nations that made it to the most World Cups. The teams that have played in at least 75% of World Cup tournaments have on average placed in the top 10 teams in the world. These teams are Brazil, Italy, Germany (including West Germany), and Argentina.

Professional League Quality

Nations that qualify for the World Cup should a squad of better players. A professional league with more players who have played in the World Cup is likely to be a better quality league. The data shows World Cup Players disproportionately play in 5 Western European professional leagues.
