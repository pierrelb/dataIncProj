Professional League Quality

Nations that qualify for the World Cup should a squad of better players. A professional league with more players who have played in the World Cup is likely to be a better quality league. The data shows World Cup Players disproportionately play in 5 Western European professional leagues. These countries 5 benefit as the players from their nation do not need to leave to play with better quality players, aiding their development. Other countries must find another way to develop their national teams.
