Player Age

A World Cup tournament squad may is expected to contain the best players available to a coach. Some are younger and more physically gifted, while others are older and depend on their experience. Discussions about squad composition usually relys on anecdotal evidence. Teams were under 25 years of age on average during the earliest World Cups. The average age rose by about 2 years since then. The age of more successful teams, and their makeup should also be investigated. For example, it would be intersting to understand if teams with players in their mid-20s performs differently from teams where player ages are more widely varied.
